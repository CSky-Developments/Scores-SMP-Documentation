ScoresSMP - A new unique gameplay for Minecraft SMP with custom items with custom abilities!

ScoresSMP is a plugin that adds special, powerful items to your server called "Scores." Players can find, craft, and level up these items to get amazing new abilities.

This plugin makes playing on your server more fun and gives players exciting goals. They will want to explore the world, fight bosses, and become stronger. It's the best way to keep players coming back every day!

Why You Should Get ScoresSMP

  • Keeps Players Online: This plugin gives your players a long-term goal. They will spend many hours trying to find and upgrade all the Scores.
  • Fun for Everyone: It doesn't matter if your players like to mine, fight monsters, or battle other players. There is a Score for every type of player.
  • Make Your Server Special: Most servers don't have items like these. ScoresSMP will make your server unique and more exciting than others.
  • Looks Amazing: Each Score has its own special look (a custom texture). When a player levels up a Score, its appearance changes to look even cooler!

How It Works for Players (The Adventure!)

The journey to become powerful is simple and fun.

  1. Find a Template: First, a player must find a "Score Template." These are rare items found in treasure chests around the world or from killing certain monsters.
  2. Craft the Score: Next, the player uses the template in a special crafting recipe with other rare materials. This creates the Level 1 Score.
  3. Level It Up: The new Score has special abilities, but they are weak at first. The item's description tells the player what challenges they need to complete (like "kill 10 zombies" or "mine 50 diamonds").
  4. Become Powerful: When a player completes the challenges, the Score levels up! It goes from Level 1, to Level 2, and finally to Level 3. With each level, the item looks cooler and its abilities become much stronger.

The Special Items: The Six Scores & Their Abilities

Here are the six main Scores your players can find and master.

The Fire Score

This Score uses the power of the Nether. It is perfect for players who love fire and explosions.

How to Obtain the Template:
  • Loot Nether Chests: Search inside chests in Nether Fortresses, Bastion Remnants, and at Ruined Portals (20% chance).
  • Hunt Nether Creatures: Defeat mobs like Blazes, Ghasts, Magma Cubes, and Piglins (5% chance).
How to Craft It: Special Abilities:
  • Fire Resistance (Left-Click): Makes you unable to be hurt by fire or lava for a short time.
  • Fireball (Right-Click): Lets you shoot a big, exploding fireball, just like a Ghast!
  • Ignite (Shift-Click): Creates a circle of fire around you, setting all nearby enemies on fire.

The Water Score

This Score uses the power of the ocean. It is great for exploring underwater.

How to Obtain the Template:
  • Search for Buried Treasure: A template is guaranteed to be inside every Buried Treasure chest!
  • Explore Sunken Ships: Search chests inside Shipwrecks (25% chance).
  • Fight Ocean Mobs: Drops from Guardians (25% chance), Elder Guardians (10% chance), and Drowned (5% chance).
How to Craft It: Special Abilities:
  • Breathing (Left-Click): Lets you breathe underwater for a long time.
  • Dolphin's Grace (Right-Click): Gives you a super-fast swimming speed.
  • Water Shield (Shift-Click): Creates a beautiful bubble of water around you. Nothing outside the bubble can hurt you!

The Mine Score

This Score is for players who love to dig and find treasure.

How to Obtain the Template:
  • Loot Dungeons and Mines: Search chests in Abandoned Mineshafts and Dungeons (20% chance).
  • Mine Rare Ores: Has a small chance to drop from mining Ancient Debris (5% chance) and Diamond Ore (1% chance).
How to Craft It: Special Abilities:
  • Haste (Left-Click): Lets you mine blocks almost instantly.
  • Cannon (Right-Click): Lets you shoot a TNT block that flies through the air before exploding.
  • Blast (Shift-Click): Creates a safe explosion around you that hurts monsters but doesn't hurt you or break blocks.

The Dragon Score

This Score uses the magical power of The End and the Ender Dragon.

How to Obtain the Template:
  • Kill the Ender Dragon: The Ender Dragon has a 50% chance to drop a template.
  • Loot End Cities: Search chests in End Cities (20% chance).
How to Craft It: Special Abilities:
  • Flight (Left-Click): Gives you the ability to fly like you are in creative mode for a limited time.
  • Dragon's Breath (Right-Click): Shoots a ball of dragon's breath that leaves a damaging magic cloud on the ground.
  • Ground (Shift-Click): Traps your enemies by teleporting them deep into the ground.

The PvP Score

This Score is for players who love to fight other players and be the strongest warrior.

How to Obtain the Template:
  • There is a 5% chance this template will drop when you kill another player.
How to Craft It: Special Abilities:
  • Strength (Left-Click): Gives you a temporary boost of attack damage.
  • Giant Sword (Right-Click): Summons a huge sword from the sky to crash down on your enemies, sending them flying.
  • Minions (Shift-Click): Summons small, armored zombie friends who fight for you.

The Warden Score

This Score uses the scary power of the Deep Dark and the Warden.

How to Obtain the Template:
  • Defeating the Warden has a 50% chance to drop a template.
  • Search chests in Ancient Cities (20% chance).
How to Craft It: Special Abilities:
  • Regeneration (Left-Click): Heals your health very quickly.
  • Sonic Boom (Right-Click): Shoots a sonic blast that can't be blocked by armor, just like the Warden.
  • Heart (Shift-Click): Gives you extra temporary hearts (absorption hearts) for protection.

The Honor Score - The Ultimate Item!

When a player has mastered all six Scores, they can create the final, most powerful item in the game.

How to Obtain It:
  • The Honor Score is special. It does not have a template to find.
  • It can only be crafted after you have mastered all six other scores.
How to Craft It: Aura of Power:

When a player holds the Honor Score, the other six scores will magically float and spin around them! It looks amazing and shows everyone that this player is a true master.

The Strongest Abilities:
  • Buff (Left-Click): Gives you many good effects at once: Speed, Strength, Regeneration, and more.
  • Swarm (Right-Click): Shoots many magic arrows that automatically chase and attack your enemies with different evil effects.
  • Shamak (Shift-Click): The ultimate attack! Creates a huge blast of dark magic that badly hurts all nearby enemies and makes the world dark and stormy for a moment.

How to Install & Get Your License Key

This is a premium plugin. You will need a license key to use it. The setup is easy!

  1. Put the ScoresSMP.jar file into your server's plugins folder.
  2. Start your server one time. It will make a new folder. Open the config.yml file inside and change Terms: false to Terms: true.
  3. Start the server again. Look in the server console. It will show you your Server IP, Port, and World UUID. Copy this information.
  4. Join our Discord server and ask for a license. Give us the server info you copied. You can join here: https://discord.gg/FxxMEaaZJj
  5. We will give you a file called license.key. Put this file into the /plugins/ScoresSMP/ folder.
  6. Restart your server one last time. The plugin will now be fully working. Enjoy!
